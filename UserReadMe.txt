User Read Me:

Swipe to transition from page to page

Your feed will only contain polls that were sent to you or that you created. Although it may seem that you can still vote on closed polls, in actuality you’ll find that your votes are ignored once a poll is closed.

The “INSTA” checkbox causes the recipients’ phones to set off a notification. You can manage who can send you notifications by going to the “Settings” page and then clicking on “Notifications”.
